About Me

I love to talk! I could have you here for an hour giving you such elaborate details of my career background, you’d probably could bring it to life with a vivid picture. So, here’s five quick things about me, to save you some time. 1. I have a background in multimedia communications, and I have experience in just about anything you can think of when it comes to media. I’ve done play by play, been a sideline reporter, editor, producer, worked in radio, and even been an anchor/host for local TV. 2. I left the freelance world with ambitions to work my way up in business management. In two short years, I’ve successfully became an assistant manager at Enterprise Truck, opening up a new branch and successfully running it from the ground up. 3. I’m a creative at heart and will take on any opportunity to exercise my artistic skills. So far, I’ve booked deals as an interior designer, events planner, video producer, and make-up artist. 4. I’m a nerd for schedules, to-do list, organization, or anything that has to do with planning in general. Without a plan, a dream is only a wish. 5. I love Jesus! There is nothing I am more passionate about than my relationship with Christ and helping people. My ultimate goal in life is to have people say, they were positively impacted by me. My motto is: Love God and Love people.
